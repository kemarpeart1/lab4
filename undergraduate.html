B.S. in Information Systems

A people‑centered computing degree that blends technology, business, and design.

What you'll learn

  • Databases (SQL, data modeling), systems analysis & design
  • Networking & security fundamentals
  • Web development, UX, and accessibility
  • Project management and teamwork

Why UMBC IS

  • Small classes with hands‑on labs
  • Internships with regional tech employers
  • Student orgs and research opportunities
  • Career‑ready portfolio projects

Admissions & Next Steps

  1. Apply to UMBC (or declare/change major to IS).
  2. Complete math placement and prerequisites.
  3. Meet with an IS advisor to plan your path.
  4. Join student orgs and start a portfolio project.
